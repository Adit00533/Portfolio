Enhancing Medical Record Security: Using Role-based Access Control, Digital Signatures, and RSA Encryption

Publication Date: Nov 2024

Publication: IEEE | Link

Medicare, the proposed web application, provides a unique way to store medical records securely by encrypting them and providing role-based access control. Doctors can view all patients' records, but patients can only view their respective records. Doctors also have the option of accessing RSA-encrypted chat, and a unique digital signature is embedded in each record to ensure the integrity of the records. This provides a better approach for medical record management. Session management and secure authentication have been implemented to track actions that occur on the website. The encrypted records are stored in MongoDB, offering a more secure and efficient way to manage and share healthcare records.
