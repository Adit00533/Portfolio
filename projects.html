Advancing Alpha-Thalassemia Carrier Screening for Better Predictions Using Explainable AI

Publication Date: Dec 2023

Publication: IEEE | Link

Developed a two-stage diagnostic approach: the first stage differentiates between normal individuals and alpha thalassemia patients, while the second stage distinguishes between silent and trait carriers of alpha thalassemia. Achieved an accuracy of 87% using the Decision Tree model, outperforming other algorithms. Incorporated explainable AI techniques, such as SHapley Additive exPlanations (SHAP) and LIME, to enhance the interpretability of the model, aiding medical practitioners in the diagnosis process.
